Our Story

charity: water was founded in 2006 with a mission to bring clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. We believe that access to clean water is a basic human right, and we work tirelessly to make this a reality for communities in need.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
